FEATURED PLAYERS

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, is a former international cricketer who captained the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the captain of India national cricket team in Tests. He plays for Delhi in domestic cricket and Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League with others.

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian international cricketer who captains the India national cricket team in ODIs and T20Is. He vice-captains the Indian cricket team in Test cricket.He plays for Mumbai.

Ravindra Jadeja

Ravindrasinh Anirudhsinh Jadeja, commonly known as Ravindra Jadeja, is an Indian international cricketer. He is an all-rounder, who bats left-handed in the middle-order and bowls left-arm orthodox spin.

Rituraj Gaikwad

Ruturaj Dashrat Gaikwad is an Indian cricketer who plays for Maharashtra in domestic cricket and for the Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League with other players of india and other countries..
